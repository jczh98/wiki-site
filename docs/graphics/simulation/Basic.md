### Material Derivative

$\frac{\mathrm{D}\mathbf{A}}{\mathrm{D}t} \equiv \frac{\partial \mathbf{A}}{\partial t} + \mathbf{u}\cdot\nabla\mathbf{A}$

$\mathbf{A}$ is vector filed and $\mathbf{u}$ is the flow velocity
